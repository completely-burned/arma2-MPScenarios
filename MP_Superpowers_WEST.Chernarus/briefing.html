In this mission your goal is to defeat the opposing side by destroying their base or finishing other victory conditions that have been set. Your side will need to earn income by capturing towns to gain supply lines. Each side has a Commander who can build a base and order teams. The Commander starts with a Mobile HQ which can deploy into a headquarters and construct the base. The base can be expanded as towns are captured and supplies are delivered. Bases can be used by players to buy equipment.

This menu can be opened by pressing 'T' or using Options from the action menu. Some options will only be available if you are near the proper facilities.
The options are:

MISSION - Main interface. Here you can select missions to earn money, call fire missions, or management waypoints. The Commander can order teams from here.

TEAM - You can buy vehicles and troops or transfer funds to other teams.

GEAR - You can buy change your personal loadout here.

HELP - Opens this screen. You can also select other help topics at the bottom.

VOTE - Vote for a new Commander. If no player gets the majority vote then an AI Commander will be selected.

BUILD - Build the base if you are the Commander. Players can also construct defense. Must be near a repair truck or friendly camp. Commander must be near the HQ or Mobile HQ.
